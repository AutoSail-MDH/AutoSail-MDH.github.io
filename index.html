AUTOSAIL MDH

AutoSail is a student project where five M.Sc.Eng students will develop an autonomous sailing boat with the goal to compete in WRSC 2021.

Who are we?

We are five Master of Science in Engineering students from Mälardalens University in Västerås, Sweden. We are working on our final project of our last year at the Robotics program. An autonomous sailing boat will be developed and the goal is to compete in WRSC(World Robotic Sailing Championships) in 2021.
